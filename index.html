Modern and Well Equipped

Abbeywood Veterinary Clinic is modern and well-equipped with full surgical and modern hospitalisation facilities. Advanced diagnostics (digital x-ray, specialist ultrasound especially heart work-up and lab equipment) are available. It specialises in the care and advice on domestic animals.

House calls

House calls may be provided on request. But, as with any visit from the veterinary surgery, not all facilities are available and the animal may have to be brought to the surgery for further investigations to gain an accurate diagnosis. Please ring as early as possible if you require a home visit.
